About Us

At 360 Fit Performance Centre, we make fitness simple for you in a affordable way.

We’re not just your average gym where it’s all about equipment and no service. We go out of our way to ensure each 360 Fit Member has the tools they need to achieve their goal. From Programme design to affordable Personal Training, we have you covered.

We even look after all of your nutritional needs with our MEAL PREP service that can provide you with all your meals cooked freshly for you, making achieving your goal, SIMPLE. Located just off Jct 3 of the m621 as you enter the heart of Leeds, it could not be easier to reach us. We are less than 10 minutes walk from Leeds Train Station and over 40 FREE Car Parking spaces for our members. If you want a gym that will actually push you to achieve your fitness goal then 360 Fit PC is the perfect place for you.

- Divesh Patel, Owner of 360 Fit Performance Centre

Our Services

  • Official Hammer Strength Training Centre

    We are an Official Hammer Strength Training Centre as well as having a range of Watson Gym equipment. This is all brand new and kept to the highest standard.

  • Capped Membership

    We have a capped membership, this means that the gym will never be over crowded so you never have to wait for equipment!

  • Free Car Parking

    We offer over 40 FREE car parking spaces and have great transport links as we are just located off jct 3 of the m621.

  • 1-2-1 Personal Training

    We have some of the best trainers around who really get to know you and your situation to help you achieve your goals!

  • 360 Fit Training APP

    Along with personal training you will recieve access to our very own app! This will allow you to track every workout as well as track the foods that you eat so you can always stay on track!

  • Olympic Lifting Zone

    We have a dedicated Olympic Lifting zone with high quality hammer Strength equipment.

  • Macro Based, Healthy Meal Prep

    We offer a meal prep service. We will prepare up to 4 meals a day 7 days a week! this takes the stress away from yourself and means you can focus on your goals!

  • Cash/Rolling Contacts

    You don't have to be tied in to a contract, we offer a monthly rolling contact which you are free to cancel at any time.

  • Rearranging equipment where possible to help with social distancing
  • Qualified Trainers on shift at all times to help you maximise your time in the gym.
  • Showers will remain closed and changes have been made to the changing rooms./li>

  • A Self Certified Health Screening Upon entry
